## New Platform Celebrates Whitstable's Independent High Street

**whitstable.shop launches as free directory for local businesses**

---

**WHITSTABLE, Kent — [DATE]** — A new digital platform dedicated entirely to Whitstable's independent shops, restaurants, and local businesses has launched today. whitstable.shop offers visitors and residents a comprehensive guide to the town's unique high street, complete with reviews, events listings, and local information.

The platform features over 80 independent businesses across Harbour Street, High Street, Oxford Street, and surrounding areas. Users can browse by category, read community reviews, save favourite shops, and discover local events—all without advertising or chain businesses.

"Whitstable's high street is special because of its independent shops," said [FOUNDER NAME], creator of whitstable.shop. "I wanted to build something that celebrates that and helps visitors discover what makes this town unique. No chains, no ads—just the local businesses that give Whitstable its character."

### Key Features Include:

- **Shop Directory**: Over 80 independent businesses with descriptions, opening hours, and contact details
- **User Reviews**: Community-driven ratings and reviews
- **Interactive Map**: Find shops and get directions
- **Events Calendar**: Local markets, festivals, and community events
- **Local Info**: Tide times, parking information, and visitor tips
- **Mobile-First Design**: Built for visitors exploring on their phones

The platform is free for both visitors and businesses. Shop owners can claim their listing to update information and respond to reviews.

whitstable.shop is now live at **https://whitstable.shop**
