## One-Paragraph Description

whitstable.shop is a free, mobile-friendly platform dedicated to Whitstable's independent high street. Featuring 80+ local shops, restaurants, and cafes, the site helps visitors discover the best of Whitstable with user reviews, an interactive map, events calendar, and practical local information like tide times and parking. No chains, no ads—just the independent businesses that make Whitstable special.

## FAQ for Journalists

**Q: How do you make money?**
A: Currently free. Future plans include optional premium features for shop owners (analytics, promotion tools). Core features will always be free.

**Q: Why only Whitstable?**
A: Starting hyperlocal allows deep knowledge and relationships. May expand to nearby towns if successful.

**Q: How do you ensure review quality?**
A: Community moderation, clear guidelines, and manual review of flagged content.

**Q: Are you affiliated with the council or tourism board?**
A: No, this is an independent project.

**Q: How do shops get listed?**
A: We added initial listings from public information. Shop owners can claim and update their listing for free.
