# Flyer Design Specification

## Overview

A6 double-sided flyer for general distribution around Whitstable.

---

## Dimensions

- **Size:** A6 (105mm × 148mm)
- **Orientation:** Portrait
- **Bleed:** 3mm all sides
- **Safe zone:** 5mm from trim edge

---

## Typography

### Primary Font
**Nunito** — Google Fonts, Open source

| Style | Weight | Size | Usage |
|-------|--------|------|-------|
| Heading | 700 (Bold) | 24pt | Main title |
| Subheading | 600 (SemiBold) | 14pt | Feature titles |
| Body | 400 (Regular) | 11pt | Body copy |
| Caption | 400 (Regular) | 9pt | Small text, URL |

### Fallback
Sans-serif system fonts if Nunito unavailable.

---

## Front Side Design

### Layout (top to bottom)

1. **Header Area** (top 20%)
   - Logo: "whitstable.shop" in Nunito Bold
   - Tagline underneath: "Your guide to Whitstable's high street"
   - Background: Shell (#F5F5F0)

2. **Main Content** (middle 50%)
   - Large QR code (minimum 35mm × 35mm)
   - Centered on page
   - Clear space around QR: minimum 5mm
   - Below QR: "Scan to explore" in Driftwood

3. **Call to Action** (bottom 30%)
   - "Discover local shops, cafés, restaurants, and events"
   - URL: whitstable.shop in Saltwater blue
   - Background: subtle wave pattern or solid Shell

### Design Notes
- Keep it clean and uncluttered
- No gradients or glows
- No stock photography
- Consider a subtle oyster shell or wave motif if needed
- The QR code is the hero element

---

## Back Side Design

### Layout (top to bottom)

1. **Header** (top 15%)
   - "What's on whitstable.shop?" in Oyster, bold

2. **Three Features** (middle 60%)

   **Feature 1: Find**
   - Icon: Simple location pin or search icon
   - "50+ local shops, cafés and restaurants"
   - "Opening hours, contact details, what's nearby"

   **Feature 2: Discover**
   - Icon: Calendar or star icon
   - "Events, markets, and what's on"
   - "Never miss a harbour market or gallery opening"

   **Feature 3: Review**
   - Icon: Speech bubble or heart icon
   - "Honest reviews from real locals"
   - "Share your favourites, discover new ones"

3. **Footer** (bottom 25%)
   - "Made in Whitstable, for Whitstable"
   - URL: whitstable.shop
   - Small text: "A community project — free for everyone"

### Design Notes
- Three equal columns or stacked sections
- Simple line icons, not filled/detailed
- Consistent icon style throughout
- Plenty of white space

---

## QR Code Specification

- **URL:** https://whitstable.shop
- **Size:** Minimum 35mm × 35mm for reliable scanning
- **Error correction:** Level H (30%)
- **Quiet zone:** 4 modules minimum (built into size requirement)
- **Colors:** Black (#1B1B1B) on white — do not use colors
- **Format:** Vector (SVG) preferred for print

---

## Print Specification

- **Paper stock:** 350gsm silk or matt coated
- **Finish:** Matt lamination on both sides (optional)
- **Quantity suggestions:** 500-1000 for initial run
- **Printing:** CMYK, high quality litho or digital

---

## File Delivery

Request the following from designer:

1. Print-ready PDF (300dpi, CMYK, 3mm bleed, crop marks)
2. Editable source file (InDesign, Illustrator, or Figma)
3. PNG exports for web/social use (RGB, no bleed)

---

## Distribution Ideas

- Stack in participating shops (ask permission)
- Local notice boards (library, community centre)
- Cafés with counter space
- B&Bs and holiday rentals
- Estate agents (for renters/buyers moving to area)
- Train station (with permission)
- Beach hut rental offices
