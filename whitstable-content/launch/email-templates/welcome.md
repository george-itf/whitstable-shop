# Welcome Email

**Trigger:** Sent immediately after user signup

---

## Subject Line Options

- Welcome to whitstable.shop
- You're in! Here's what you can do on whitstable.shop
- Welcome, neighbour

**Recommended:** Welcome to whitstable.shop

---

## Email Body

---

**Welcome to whitstable.shop**

Thanks for signing up.

Here's what you can do now:

**Save your favourites**
Found a great café? Heart it. Building a list of places to try? Save them all in one place.

**Leave reviews**
Tried somewhere brilliant (or not so brilliant)? Share your experience. Honest reviews help everyone.

**Get updates**
We'll occasionally email about new features and what's happening in Whitstable. Not often, we promise.

**Explore:**
→ [Browse all shops](https://whitstable.shop/shops)
→ [See what's on](https://whitstable.shop/events)
→ [Find somewhere open now](https://whitstable.shop/shops?open=true)

That's it. Simple.

Questions? Just reply to this email.

Cheers,
whitstable.shop

---

*You're receiving this because you signed up at whitstable.shop. [Unsubscribe](#) if you'd rather not hear from us.*

---

## Design Notes

- Plain text style, minimal HTML
- Single column, mobile-friendly
- No images required (loads faster, feels more personal)
- Links as text, not buttons
- Keep it short — people skim emails
