# School Term Dates

## Overview

Schools in Whitstable follow the **Kent County Council** academic calendar. Most state schools in the area use these dates, though some academies and independent schools may vary slightly.

## INSET Days

Schools set their own INSET (teacher training) days — typically 5 per year. These vary between schools.

Check with your specific school for their INSET dates.

## Impact on Visitors

School holidays mean:
- **Beaches and town are busier**
- **Parking is more difficult**
- **Restaurants may need booking**
- **Family-oriented events increase**

If you're visiting without children and prefer quieter times, term-time weekdays are best.
