# Bin Collection in Whitstable

## Overview

Whitstable is part of the Canterbury City Council area. Bin collections are fortnightly, alternating between recycling and general waste.

## Collection Days

Your collection day depends on your street. Most of Whitstable is collected on either **Tuesday** or **Wednesday**.

To find your specific collection day:
- Visit [Canterbury City Council's bin collection lookup](https://www.canterbury.gov.uk/bins-and-recycling/bin-collection-days)
- Enter your postcode (CT5 for most of Whitstable, CT6 for Tankerton area)

## What Goes Where

### Blue Bin (Recycling)
Collected fortnightly.

**Yes:**
- Paper and cardboard (flattened)
- Plastic bottles (rinsed, lids on)
- Tins and cans (rinsed)
- Glass bottles and jars (rinsed)
- Aerosols (empty)
- Cartons (Tetra Pak)

**No:**
- Plastic bags or film
- Food waste
- Textiles
- Batteries
- Polystyrene

### Grey Bin (General Waste)
Collected fortnightly, opposite week to recycling.

Everything that can't be recycled goes here. Keep it bagged to prevent mess.

### Green Bin (Garden Waste)
**Optional paid service** - £45 per year (2024 prices).

Collected fortnightly March to November, monthly December to February.

**Yes:**
- Grass cuttings
- Hedge trimmings
- Leaves
- Weeds
- Small branches

**No:**
- Soil
- Rubble
- Treated wood
- Food waste

### Food Waste Caddy
Collected weekly. Small brown caddy lined with compostable bags.

**Yes:**
- All food scraps
- Tea bags and coffee grounds
- Meat and fish (including bones)
- Dairy products
- Eggshells

## Bank Holiday Changes

When a bank holiday falls on your collection day, collections are usually delayed by one day for the rest of that week. Check the council website for specific dates around Christmas and Easter.

## Missed Collection

If your bin wasn't collected:
- Check it was out by 6:30am
- Check it wasn't contaminated with wrong items
- Report it within 48 hours via the council website

## Bulky Waste

For large items (furniture, appliances), book a bulky waste collection:
- Phone: 01227 862000
- Online: Canterbury City Council website
- Charges apply

## Helpful Tips

- Put bins out the night before (collection starts at 6:30am)
- Don't overfill bins — lids must close
- In windy weather, face bins away from the road
- Rinse food containers — dirty recycling gets rejected
