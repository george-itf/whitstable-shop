# Council Contacts

## Overview

Whitstable is part of the **Canterbury City Council** district and **Kent County Council**. Different councils handle different services.

- **Canterbury City Council:** Local services — bins, parking, planning, council tax
- **Kent County Council:** County-wide services — roads, schools, social care

## Local Councillors

Whitstable is covered by several wards within Canterbury City Council:

### Whitstable Wards

#### Gorrell Ward
Covers central Whitstable around the High Street and harbour.

#### Harbour Ward
Covers the harbour area and parts of the seafront.

#### Tankerton Ward
Covers Tankerton and the eastern end of town.

### Find Your Councillor
Use the council's ward finder:
- **Online:** [canterbury.gov.uk/councillors](https://www.canterbury.gov.uk/councillors)
- Enter your postcode to find your local councillor's contact details

### Kent County Council Division
Whitstable falls within the **Whitstable** division of Kent County Council.

Find your county councillor at [kent.gov.uk/councillors](https://www.kent.gov.uk/about-the-council/councillors-and-democracy)
