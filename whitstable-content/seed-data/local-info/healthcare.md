## Out of Hours / Urgent Care

### NHS 111
**Phone:** 111 (free, 24/7)
**Online:** [111.nhs.uk](https://111.nhs.uk)

For urgent medical help when your GP is closed:
- Advice on symptoms
- Nearest open pharmacy
- Referral to out-of-hours GP if needed
- Direction to A&E if necessary

### Minor Injuries Unit
**Location:** Estuary View Medical Centre, Boorman Way, Whitstable CT5 3SE
**Phone:** 01227 284353

For minor injuries that don't need A&E:
- Cuts requiring stitches
- Minor burns
- Sprains and strains
- Minor fractures
- Bites and stings

**Call first** to check availability and current wait times.

### Nearest A&E
**Kent & Canterbury Hospital**
Ethelbert Road, Canterbury CT1 3NG
**Phone:** 01227 766877

Approximately 15 minutes drive from Whitstable.

For life-threatening emergencies, always call **999**.

## Visitors & Holiday Makers

If you need medical care while visiting Whitstable:

1. **Minor issues:** Visit a pharmacy for advice
2. **Urgent care:** Call NHS 111
3. **Emergency:** Call 999 or go to A&E

You can be treated by any NHS GP as a temporary patient if you're staying in the area for less than 3 months. Call the surgery to arrange.

EU visitors should carry their EHIC/GHIC card. Other overseas visitors should have travel insurance.
