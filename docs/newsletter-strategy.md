# Newsletter Strategy

"The Whitstable Weekly" - a value-packed, 2-minute read sent every Friday.

---

## Newsletter Overview

**Name**: The Whitstable Weekly

**Frequency**: Every Friday at 9am

**Goal**: Keep subscribers engaged and drive return visits

**Platform**: Buttondown (privacy-focused, simple)

**Target Length**: 2-minute read maximum

---

## Content Structure

### Section 1: What's On This Weekend

**Purpose**: Immediate value for planning

**Content**:
- 2-3 local events happening this weekend
- Brief descriptions (1-2 sentences each)
- Links to full details

**Example**:
> ## This Weekend in Whitstable
>
> **Saturday**: Harbour Market, 9am-2pm. Fresh produce, artisan goods, and great coffee.
>
> **Sunday**: Live music at The Twelve Taps from 4pm. Free entry.
>
> [See all events →](https://whitstable.shop/events)

---

### Section 2: Shop of the Week

**Purpose**: Featured discovery

**Content**:
- One shop spotlight
- What makes them special
- One specific recommendation
- Link to their listing

**Example**:
> ## Shop of the Week: Frank
>
> This tiny menswear shop on Harbour Street is a hidden gem. Owner James sources unique pieces you won't find anywhere else in Kent.
>
> **Try**: Ask about their Japanese denim selection
>
> [View Frank on whitstable.shop →](https://whitstable.shop/shop/frank)

---

### Section 3: Tide Times

**Purpose**: Practical local info

**Content**:
- Saturday and Sunday high tide times
- Brief beach tip

**Example**:
> ## Weekend Tides
>
> **Saturday**: High 10:34am, Low 4:52pm
> **Sunday**: High 11:22am, Low 5:38pm
>
> Pro tip: Low tide is best for the beach huts stretch!

---

### Section 4: New on the Site

**Purpose**: Show activity and growth

**Content**:
- New shops added this week
- New features launched
- Popular reviews

**Example**:
> ## New This Week
>
> - **3 new shops**: The Whitstable Pantry, Coastal Crafts, Samphire
> - **12 new reviews** from the community
> - **New feature**: Filter by "open now"

---

### Section 5: From the Community

**Purpose**: Social proof and engagement

**Content**:
- Quote from a recent review
- User photo (with permission)
- Community highlight

**Example**:
> ## From the Community
>
> *"Best coffee in Whitstable, hands down. The team are lovely too."*
> — Review of The Whitstable Grind
>
> [Read more reviews →](https://whitstable.shop/reviews)

## Subject Line Strategy

### Formulas

1. **Event hook**: "Whitstable Farmers Market is back this Saturday"
2. **Discovery**: "Have you tried [shop name]?"
3. **Seasonal**: "Your Christmas shopping guide"
4. **Curiosity**: "The shop locals keep secret"
5. **Practical**: "Weekend tides + 3 events you'll love"

### Examples

- "This weekend: Live music, markets & [shop] spotlight"
- "Rain forecast? Here's where to go in Whitstable"
- "New: 5 shops added + your weekend guide"
- "The coffee shop everyone's talking about"

## Best Practices

### Do

- Keep it short (2 minutes max)
- Front-load the best content
- Be consistent (every Friday, same time)
- Make it scannable
- Include one clear CTA
- Test subject lines

### Don't

- Pad with filler content
- Send more than weekly (at first)
- Make it all promotional
- Use clickbait that disappoints
- Forget mobile readers
