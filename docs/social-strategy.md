# Social Media Strategy

A practical guide to building whitstable.shop's social presence and community.

---

## Platform Strategy

### Primary: Instagram (@whitstable.shop)

**Why Instagram**:
- Visual platform suits shop/food content
- Strong local community engagement
- Stories for real-time updates
- Reels for reach and discovery

**Target Audience**:
- Whitstable visitors (day-trippers, tourists)
- Local residents
- Shop owners
- Food/lifestyle enthusiasts

### Secondary: Facebook

**Why Facebook**:
- Reach older demographics
- Local groups are active
- Events integration
- Business-friendly features

**Approach**: Repurpose Instagram content, engage in local groups

### Not Priority (Yet)

- **Twitter/X**: Lower local engagement
- **TikTok**: Resource-intensive, future consideration
- **LinkedIn**: Not relevant for B2C local

## Posting Schedule

### Frequency

- **Feed posts**: 4x per week
- **Stories**: Daily (or near-daily)
- **Reels**: 1-2x per week (when capacity allows)

### Best Times (Test and Adjust)

- **Weekday mornings**: 8-9am (commute scrolling)
- **Lunch**: 12-1pm (lunch break browsing)
- **Evenings**: 6-8pm (planning visits)
- **Weekend mornings**: 9-11am (trip planning)

### Weekly Template

| Day | Content Type |
|-----|--------------|
| Monday | Shop Spotlight |
| Tuesday | Local Info / Tips |
| Wednesday | Behind the Scenes / Personal |
| Thursday | Shop Spotlight |
| Friday | Weekend Events / What's On |
| Saturday | User Content / Community |
| Sunday | Relaxed content / Rest |

## Engagement Guidelines

### Response Times

- Comments: Within 4 hours during active hours
- DMs: Within 24 hours
- Mentions: Within 24 hours

### Tone of Voice

- Friendly and warm
- Local knowledge, not tourist brochure
- Helpful without being salesy
- Genuine enthusiasm
- Admit when you don't know something

### Engagement Actions

Daily:
- [ ] Respond to all comments
- [ ] Reply to DMs
- [ ] Like/comment on tagged posts
- [ ] Engage with local accounts

Weekly:
- [ ] Engage with shop social accounts
- [ ] Comment on local hashtags
- [ ] Support other local initiatives

### What NOT to Do

- Don't get into arguments
- Don't be defensive about criticism
- Don't spam groups or other accounts
- Don't buy followers or engagement
- Don't post without value
