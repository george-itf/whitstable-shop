# Event Organiser Outreach Template

## Email Template for Event Organisers

---

**Target**: Market organisers, festival committees, community event planners

---

### Subject Line Options

1. "Free event promotion on whitstable.shop"
2. "List your Whitstable event for free"
3. "Help visitors discover your event"

---

### Email Template

**To**: [Event organiser email]
**Subject**: Free event promotion on whitstable.shop

---

Hi [Name / Team],

I wanted to reach out about [Event Name] and offer free promotion through whitstable.shop.

**What is whitstable.shop?**

It's a free, mobile-friendly guide to Whitstable's independent shops and local events. We help visitors discover what's happening in town.

**What we're offering:**

- **Free event listing** with full details, images, and links
- **Featured placement** on our events page
- **Social media promotion** to our followers
- **Map integration** so visitors can find the venue

**What we need from you:**

- Event name and description
- Date, time, and location
- Contact information or ticket link
- Any images or logos you'd like us to use

**Why this helps:**

Visitors to whitstable.shop are actively looking for things to do. Listing your event puts it in front of people already planning their Whitstable trip.

**No catch:**

This is genuinely free. We're building whitstable.shop to be a comprehensive guide to Whitstable, and events are a big part of what makes the town special.

**How to submit:**

Simply reply to this email with your event details, or submit directly at whitstable.shop/submit-event (if this feature exists).

Let me know if you have any questions!

Best,
[Your name]
whitstable.shop

## Event Listing Information Needed

**Required:**
- Event name
- Date and time (start and end)
- Location (venue name and address)
- Brief description (2-3 sentences)
- Organiser contact or website

**Optional but helpful:**
- Event image or poster
- Ticket information/link
- Price (free or cost)
- Age restrictions
- Accessibility info

---

## Ongoing Relationship

Once an event is listed:

1. **Before**: Promote on social media closer to date
2. **During**: Feature prominently on events page
3. **After**: Reach out for next occurrence

### Build relationships with:

- Market organisers (regular events)
- Festival committees (annual events)
- Venue managers (ongoing programming)
- Community groups (one-off events)

---

## Cross-Promotion Opportunities

**For larger events, offer:**

- Featured homepage placement
- Social media countdown
- Instagram stories
- Newsletter feature (when launched)

**In exchange, ask for:**

- "Find local shops on whitstable.shop" mention
- Link on their event page
- Social media mention
- Flyer inclusion (if physical)

---

## Notes

- Always be helpful, not pushy
- Respect their time and processes
- Follow up once, then leave it
- Build long-term relationships
- Track which organisers respond well
