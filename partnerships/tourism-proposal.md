## Executive Summary

whitstable.shop is a free, mobile-first platform helping visitors discover Whitstable's independent shops, restaurants, and local businesses. We propose a partnership that benefits both visitors and the local tourism ecosystem.

---

## About whitstable.shop

### What We Offer

- **Comprehensive Directory**: 80+ independent businesses across all categories
- **User Reviews**: Community-driven ratings and honest feedback
- **Interactive Map**: Mobile-friendly exploration tool
- **Events Calendar**: Local markets, festivals, and happenings
- **Local Information**: Tide times, parking, transport—practical visitor needs
- **Mobile-First Design**: Built for visitors exploring on their phones

### Our Audience

- Day-trippers from London (primary)
- Tourists visiting Kent coast
- Domestic UK visitors
- Whitstable residents
- Food and lifestyle enthusiasts

### Key Differentiators

| Feature | whitstable.shop | Generic Platforms |
|---------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Whitstable-exclusive | Yes | No |
| Independent businesses only | Yes | No |
| Local context (tides, parking) | Yes | No |
| No advertising | Yes | No |
| Community-driven | Yes | Limited |
