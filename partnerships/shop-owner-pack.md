## Tips for a Great Listing

### Write a Compelling Description

**Instead of**:
> "We are a coffee shop in Whitstable."

**Try**:
> "Cosy harbourside coffee shop serving single-origin espresso and homemade cakes. Perfect for people-watching with a flat white after a beach walk."

### Add Great Photos

- Show your shop front (helps people find you)
- Feature your best products
- Capture the atmosphere
- Use natural light when possible
- Aim for 3-5 quality images

### Keep Hours Updated

Nothing frustrates visitors more than arriving at a closed shop. Update your hours for:

- Bank holidays
- Seasonal changes
- Special events
- Unexpected closures

## Encouraging Reviews

Reviews help visitors discover your shop. Here are some ethical ways to encourage them:

### What You CAN Do

- Ask satisfied customers to share their experience
- Add a small sign: "Find us on whitstable.shop"
- Include your listing link in email signatures
- Share your whitstable.shop page on social media

### What You SHOULD NOT Do

- Offer incentives for reviews (against our guidelines)
- Ask only happy customers
- Write fake reviews
- Ask friends/family to write reviews

### Suggested Ask

> "If you enjoyed your visit today, we'd love if you could leave a review on whitstable.shop. It really helps other visitors find us!"

---

## Responding to Reviews

### Positive Reviews

Keep it simple and genuine:

> "Thanks so much for the kind words! Glad you enjoyed the [specific item]. Hope to see you again soon."

### Constructive Reviews

Address concerns professionally:

> "Thank you for your feedback. We're sorry [specific issue] didn't meet expectations. We're [action you're taking]. Please feel free to contact us directly at [email] so we can make it right."

### Negative Reviews

Stay calm and professional:

> "We're sorry to hear about your experience. This isn't the standard we aim for. Please contact us at [email] so we can understand what happened and make it right."

**Remember**: Don't argue publicly. Take it offline if needed.

### Common Questions

**Q: How do I change my business name?**
A: Email us and we'll update it for you.

**Q: Can I remove a negative review?**
A: We only remove reviews that violate our guidelines. Negative opinions based on genuine experiences won't be removed, but you can respond to them.

**Q: How much does it cost?**
A: Your listing is free. We may offer optional premium features in the future, but the core listing will always be free.

**Q: Can I see who saved my shop?**
A: No, saves are private to users. You can see the total count but not individual users.
